Hello everyone. This mod was previously removed, but is now back on the workshop.
I have no plans on updating this mod, and I'm making it open source.
Feel free to take anything from this mod and use it in your own.

This mod aims to feel as "Re-Logic" as possible and fully flesh out the sentry subclass.
The goal is to close or seriously remedy every gap in progression.

Additions:
- 17 sentries*
- 3 armor sets
- 3 new helmets for existing armor sets
- 4 accessories
- 1 potion
- and many sentry-related mechanics
*the crimson rod and nimbus rod have been turned into sentries
